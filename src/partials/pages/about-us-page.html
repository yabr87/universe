About the Galaxia Research Consortium (GRC)

The Galaxia Research Consortium (GRC) is an independent organization comprised of leading scientists, researchers, and experts dedicated to reviewing, validating, and disseminating the findings produced by Galaxia. With no direct affiliation to Galaxia itself, GRC’s mission is to ensure that all groundbreaking discoveries are meticulously verified and responsibly communicated to both the scientific community and the public—offering an unbiased lens through which to view our place in the cosmos.

GRC serves as a bridge between revolutionary data and scientific consensus, ensuring that new evidence is rigorously scrutinized before it reshapes our understanding of the universe.
