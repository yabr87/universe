What’s the Galaxia Research Consortium (GRC)?

The Galaxia Research Consortium (GRC) is an independent body comprised of leading scientists and experts. While not the creator of Galaxia, the GRC’s primary role is to validate, review, and disseminate its findings. The Consortium ensures that all discoveries made by Galaxia are rigorously verified and shared responsibly, offering an unbiased perspective on groundbreaking research into the potential existence of life beyond Earth.
