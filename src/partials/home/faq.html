What is Project Galaxia?

Project Galaxia is the most advanced AI initiative of its kind, designed to analyze vast amounts of astronomical data, interpret complex radio signals, and uncover potential signs of life beyond Earth. Using advanced technology, it aims to push the boundaries of what we know about the universe and reveal hidden truths that science alone couldn’t.
